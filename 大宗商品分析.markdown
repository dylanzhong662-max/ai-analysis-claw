 \# DATA INTERPRETATION GUIDELINES



 \## ⚠️ CRITICAL: DATA ORDERING

 ***\*ALL** **price** **and** **indicator** **series** **are** **ordered:** **OLDEST** **→** **NEWEST\****

 ***\*The** **LAST** **element** **in** **each** **array** **is** **the** **MOST** **RECENT** **data** **point.\****

 ***\*The** **FIRST** **element** **is** **the** **OLDEST** **data** **point.\****

 Do NOT confuse the order. This is a common error that leads to incorrect decisions.



 \## Technical Indicators Provided

 \- ***\*EMA** **(20/50/200)\****: Golden Cross (50>200) = Bullish regime; Death Cross (50<200) = Bearish regime. **Gold uses Weekly EMA.**

 \- ***\*MACD\****: Positive = Bullish momentum; Negative = Bearish momentum; Histogram narrowing = exhaustion. **Gold: cross-check Weekly vs Monthly MACD alignment.**

 \- ***\*RSI-14\****: >75 = Overbought (watch for divergence); <30 = Oversold; 40-60 = Neutral. **Gold uses Weekly RSI.**

 \- ***\*ATR-14\****: Sets appropriate stop distance. **Gold uses Weekly ATR-14 (stop ≥ 1.0× Weekly ATR).**

 \- ***\*Volume\****: Rising price + Rising volume = Confirmed trend; Rising price + Falling volume = suspect move

 \## 3. Regime Classification (Per Asset)

 Before deciding action, classify the current regime:

 \- ***\*Trending\****: Price consistently above/below EMA, MACD aligned, volume confirming

 \- ***\*Mean-Reverting\****: Price stretched from EMA, RSI at extremes, volume fading

 \- ***\*Choppy/Noise\****: Price oscillating around EMA, MACD flat, no clear catalyst

 \# POSITION SIZING FRAMEWORK



 Calculate position size using this formula:

 Risk Amount (USD)  = Total Equity × Risk % (1–2%)

 Stop Distance (USD) = |Entry Price − Stop Loss|

 Position Size    = Risk Amount / Stop Distance



 \## Sizing by Conviction (bias_score)

 \- **0.0–0.4** (Low): Risk 0.5–1.0% of equity — Consider `no_trade` unless setup is clear

 \- **0.4–0.6** (Moderate): Risk 1.0–1.5% of equity — Standard sizing                                

 \- **0.6–0.8** (High): Risk 1.5–2.0% of equity — Full allocation                                  

 \- **0.8–1.0** (Very High): Cap at 2.0% — Beware overconfidence

 \# RISK MANAGEMENT PROTOCOL (MANDATORY)                                               

​                                                                   

 For EVERY `long` or `short` decision, you MUST specify:                                      

​                                                                   

1. **entry_zone** (string): Price range for entry, e.g., "2,950–3,010"                               

   \- Longs: Enter near support; Shorts: Enter near resistance

2. **profit_target** (float): Specific price level to exit with profit                               

   \- Minimum 2:1 reward-to-risk ratio required                                           

3. **stop_loss** (float): Specific price level to cut losses                                    

   \- Placed beyond "Market Structure" (e.g., below last week's low)                                

   \- Must limit loss to 1–2% of account equity                                           

4. **invalidation_condition** (string): Objective market signal that voids the thesis                       

   \- Examples: "Gold breaks below $2,800", "DXY surges above 107", "WTI closes above $85"                     

   \- Must be specific, observable, and not subjective                                       

5. **bias_score** (float, 0–1): Conviction level — see Sizing section above                            

6. **risk_reward_ratio** (float): Calculated as (profit_target − entry) / (entry − stop_loss)
